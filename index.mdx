# Welcome to Navina

Navina is an AI-powered platform that transforms chaotic patient data into a clear, actionable Patient Portrait. Our goal is to empower healthcare professionals with the tools they need to provide the best possible care.

This knowledge base is a comprehensive, internal resource for all teams at Navina. Whether you're in sales, customer success, support, product, or engineering, you'll find the information you need to understand our products, serve our customers, and collaborate effectively.
